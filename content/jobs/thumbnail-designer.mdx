I currently create all of my thumbnails by myself. I'm looking for someone who can help me design compelling, scroll-stopping thumbnails on a regular basis.

I'm aiming for 2-4 videos per month where you'd have 2-3 days turnaround per thumbnail, with revisions.

You'll be an integral part of the channel and help define the visual identity as we experiment with new styles of content — coding, game dev, and builder-focused videos.

#### About you

- You have a strong portfolio of YouTube thumbnails or similar graphic design work
- You understand what makes a thumbnail click-worthy — composition, contrast, emotion, text hierarchy
- You're responsive and comfortable with fast iteration based on feedback
- You're passionate about tech, gaming, or creator content
- You're entrepreneurial and eager to be a part of building something for the long-term

#### Location

This is a fully remote position.

#### Compensation

This is a contract position and you'll be paid a flat fee per thumbnail.

#### How to apply

Fill out the application form [here](https://caccamise.link/thumbnail-apply). Include your portfolio and 2-3 example thumbnails you'd create for recent videos on the channel.
